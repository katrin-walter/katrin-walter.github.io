Professional Summary

I am a molecular biologist who turns next-generation sequencing data into clean, scalable workflows. With over 15 years at the bench, I bridge wet lab and computational analysis, building pipelines and protocols that non-bioinformatic colleagues can use independently. I am constantly learning, with a strong focus on usability, reproducibility, and translating complex science into practical tools.
